About Hollywave

Mission & Vision

Mission: To create a safe and nurturing environment for children and vulnerable youth to grow into responsible adults through holistic care and support.

Vision: Safe spaces where every child eats, learns, receives care, and grows in love — equipped to chase their dreams and build a stronger Kenya.

Core Values

  • God-Fearing: build strong moral foundations.
  • Compassion: serve with love and empathy.
  • Dignity: respect every child.
  • Teamwork: collaborate for lasting change.
  • Empowerment: education and skills for sustainable growth.
  • Discipline: nurture responsibility.
  • Excellence: deliver our best for every child.
